# Aftra -- Logo

> Source: Brand Identity Guidelines v20250428, Section 1.0

## Logo Mark

A circular form intersected by lines and nodes -- fusion of organic growth and technological precision. Inspired by Norse runes and Viking shields.

- Rendered in **Gunpowder** (`#3F3D56`) on light backgrounds, **white** on dark.
- Minimum size: **16mm print / 48px digital**
- Clearspace: the **x-height of the "a"** in the wordmark defines the minimum distance around the lockup.

## Lockup Variants (from brand guide)

1. **Horizontal lockup** -- mark left, "aftra" wordmark right (primary, used across all mediums)
2. **Contained mark** -- mark inside a rounded-rectangle container
3. **Stacked** -- mark above wordmark

All work on both light (`#F8F8FA`) and dark (`#3F3D56` / `#232230`) backgrounds. On dark backgrounds, logo switches to white.

## Logo Treatment Rules

Do NOT:
- Distort, warp, or rotate the logo
- Outline or add effects (shadows, glows)
- Change tone, tint, or opacity
- Use off-brand colors
- Add elements or taglines to the logo
- Change the typeface of the wordmark
- Alter individual parts of the mark

These rules apply to both the mark and the wordmark -- no exceptions.
