# Aftra -- Brand Overview

> Source: Brand Identity Guidelines v20250428 (`_a-torg/brandguide.pdf`)

## The Company

**Aftra** is an Icelandic cybersecurity company specializing in External Attack Surface Management (EASM). Born as a passion project among security experts at Syndis, originally named **VikingR**. The logo draws from runes and Viking shields -- protection against cyber threats with a nod to the company's Icelandic roots.

- **Website**: aftra.io
- **Legal entity**: Kolkrabbi Vinnustofa
- **Certification**: ISO/IEC 27001

## Mission & Vision

| | |
|---|---|
| **Mission** | To help companies understand and minimize their attack surface. |
| **Vision** | To make cybersecurity a part of executives' everyday life. |

## What the Product Does

Aftra is a SaaS EASM platform. It maps an organization's external digital footprint -- domains, subdomains, cloud services, APIs, shadow IT, leaked credentials -- and surfaces vulnerabilities through continuous, agentless scanning.

Key capabilities: asset discovery, vulnerability scanning, digital footprint tracking, web app / API scanning, security scoring (real-time KPI), executive dashboards, compliance reporting (NIS2, DORA).

Setup takes minutes. No agents to install. Starts at EUR 490/month.

## Target Personas

1. **C-Suite / Executives** -- peace of mind, liability awareness, KPI dashboards, business-language reporting.
2. **IT Teams** -- time efficiency, automated discovery, integration with existing workflows.
3. **Security Teams** -- continuous detection, prioritized remediation, compliance reporting.

## Tone of Voice

> "We communicate with clarity and confidence, using positive and hopeful language. We don't rely on fear-based messaging. We believe in empowering our audience."

| Our tone is | We are | We are NOT |
|---|---|---|
| Down to earth | Modern & cool | Traditional & old fashioned |
| Authentic | Polished & professional | Sloppy |
| Professional | Humble & approachable | Arrogant |
| | Fun, but serious where it counts | Boring |

Key messaging patterns:
- Frame security as **manageable and measurable**, never as crisis response
- Lead with **clarity and action** -- "actionable," "goal-driven," "prioritized"
- Translate technical risk into **business terms** (KPIs, ROI, accountability)
- Recurring motif: things "leaking," "lurking," "ignored" -- Aftra reveals unseen risk
